About

A lab built on genuine curiosity

Hypothesis Labs is an independent research practice focused on the intersection of generative AI, organizational systems, and emergent behavior. Each project starts with a specific question — not a predetermined answer.

The work here is exploratory by design. Some experiments confirm intuitions. Many don't. The findings, surprises, and dead ends are all part of the record.

The goal isn't to produce polished products. It's to develop genuine understanding of how complex systems — artificial and human — compose, communicate, and occasionally surprise us.

Current Focus
Generative agent architectures for organizational simulation — exploring how hierarchy, culture, and information flow can be modeled with modern AI.
